Ben ce

[Bence Barens]

1. Designer, barista & student.

I'm an 18-year-old multidisciplinary graphic designer based in Amsterdam. As a young designer I deliver youthful and dynamic designs, prepairing you for the future. I am always looking for new challenges.

I am currently in my first year of studying Communication and Multimedia Design at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences. The remainer of my time, I spend by helping as many people as possible with shaping their ideas. For this I use knowledge from my education, but also mainly from my many years of freelance experience. Furthermore, I am also a part-time barista and mainly a full-time coffee lover.

I am selective in the projects I accept. I make sure that I fully support the project and that we are on the same page. This way, I can make sure that I do justice to the assignment. I am also proud that I do not use AI anywhere in my design process, since that is the only way I can guarantee unique designs that truly match the vision of my clients.
