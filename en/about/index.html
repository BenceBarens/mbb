Ben ce

[Bence Barens]

1. Designer, barista & student.

I'm an 19-year-old multidisciplinary graphic designer based in Amsterdam. As a young designer I deliver youthful and dynamic designs, prepairing you for the future. I am always looking for new challenges.

I am currently in my second year of studying Communication and Multimedia Design at the Amsterdam University of Applied Sciences (AUAS), having obtained my propaedeutic certificate with distinction (cum laude). The remainder of my time, I spend by helping as many people as possible with shaping their ideas. For this I use knowledge from my education, but also mainly from my many years of freelance experience. Furthermore, I am also a part-time barista and mainly a full-time coffee lover.

I am selective in the projects I accept. I make sure that I fully support the project and that we are on the same page. This way, I can make sure that I do justice to the assignment. I am also proud that I don't use AI (artificial intelligence) for any creative work, since that is the only way I can guarantee unique designs that truly match the vision of my clients.
